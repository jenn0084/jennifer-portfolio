Chal-Rang De

Painting Slums
November 2017
5 weeks - Client project: Karvy activation, Mumbai India


Chal Rang De - Lets fill Colors. The slums of mumabi were being painted with colors. A campaign is set for people to join for being a part of this noble cause. CHAL RANG The pictures of slums were taken and colors were added using photoshop to showcase how everyone can contribute towards making the slums colorful.
